What is event photography?

Black and White Photography is a simplistic form of art. Displaying the range of shades of absolute black, all the way up to absolute white, it highlights the edges and contours of any photo it pictures. Black and White photography is best used to depict history, simplicity, to define one clear and distinct emotion of its subject.
Ask around for opportunities to photograph public (or private events!), even offering your time and services for free is a great start to getting experience in photography.


    Places you can start your event photography journey:

  • At a family/friend gathering
  • At a festival
  • At a workshop
  • At a wedding
  • At your school or workplace
  • At public events
