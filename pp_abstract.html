What is nature photography?

Abstract Photography is a unique area of photography which primarily challenges photographers to be creative. Whether that be with their shooting techniques or in their editing software, this experimental side of photography creates an endless realm of possibility to the eyes and mind -- with the ability to produce fascinating, intriguing, and astonishing results -- it is whatever the artist chooses for their image to convey.

As mentioned before, ultilizing editing software to emulate extremely abstract images of art is one of the most important and greatest aspects of this type of art form. Distort the color, saturation, hue, shape, form, gradient -- all such things -- can help you create something completely bizarre and new. Abstract photography also often incoporates the many rules and practices of art and photography. There is a plethora use for focusing on lines, symmetry, color, perspective, and many of theories and principles taught in this discipline of art.

Abstract photography is also one of Elizabeth's favorite forms of photography!


    Places you can start your abstract photography journey:

  • Close up shots on random objects
  • Places of great ambiance like the sky or water
  • Living organisms such as the silhouette of a person, animal, or plants
  • Differents forms of matter: Solids, Liquids, Gases
  • Anywhere! Just think creatively, outwardly, ABSTRACTLY. Art is subjective, afterall ;)
