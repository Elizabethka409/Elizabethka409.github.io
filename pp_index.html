Portfolio Categories

Learn about the different types of photography through galleries organized by Elizabeth!

Nature Photography

Nature Photography. One of the most common and easy accessible subjects any person wishing to capture the beauty of life has in our human reach. Although not much can be fabricated in this field of photography, the circle of life and the existence of mother nature itself is enough to be an eye candy subject of image to many, making it a great place to start one's photography journey.

Event Photography

Event Photography is a professionally well-known form of photography, which, as the name suggests, takes place during events. These places could be weddings, festivals, conventions, and workshops. Event photography is one of the best beginner photographers jobs to try out, as you get to work with real subjects (people), and learn how to adjust yourself to the profession and your camera skills.

Abstract Photography

Abstract Photography is a unique area of photography which primarily challenges photographers to be creative. Whether that be with their shooting techniques or in their editing software, this experimental side of photography creates an endless realm of possibility to the eyes and mind -- with the ability to produce amazing, intriguing, and beautiful results -- it is whatever the artist chooses for their image to convey.

Black and White Photography

Black and White Photography is a simplistic form of art. Displaying the range of shades of absolute black, all the way up to absolute white, it highlights the edges and contours of any photo it pictures. Black and White photography is best used to depict history, simplicity, to define one clear and distinct emotion of its subject.
